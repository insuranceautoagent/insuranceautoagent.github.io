The Best Insurance Agent Podcasts to Follow

Home / Blog / Blog Details

The insurance industry is evolving at lightning speed, and staying ahead requires continuous learning. Whether you're a seasoned agent or just starting, podcasts are an excellent way to absorb insights, trends, and strategies on the go. From AI-driven underwriting to climate change risks, today’s top insurance podcasts tackle the most pressing issues in the field.

Here’s a curated list of the best insurance agent podcasts to follow in 2024—each offering unique perspectives, expert interviews, and actionable advice.

Top Insurance Industry Podcasts for Agents

1. The Digital Broker

Hosted by: Ryan Deeds

Why Listen?
This podcast dives deep into how technology is reshaping the insurance landscape. From insurtech startups to blockchain applications in claims processing, The Digital Broker keeps agents informed on the latest innovations.

Key Topics Covered:
- AI and machine learning in underwriting
- Cybersecurity insurance trends
- How to leverage automation in client onboarding

2. The Insurance Guys Podcast

Hosted by: Bill Schoeffler and Christopher Garlasco

Why Listen?
A no-nonsense podcast that breaks down complex insurance topics into digestible discussions. The hosts bring decades of experience and aren’t afraid to challenge industry norms.

Key Topics Covered:
- The impact of inflation on policy pricing
- Regulatory changes affecting independent agents
- Strategies for selling in a competitive market

3. Agency Nation Radio

Hosted by: Ryan Hanley

Why Listen?
Focused on helping independent agents grow their businesses, Agency Nation Radio offers practical marketing and sales techniques.

Key Topics Covered:
- Social media marketing for insurance agents
- Building a referral-based business
- The future of remote insurance sales

Podcasts Addressing Global Insurance Challenges

4. InsureTech Geek

Hosted by: James York and Nick Lamparelli

Why Listen?
If you’re curious about how technology is disrupting insurance, this podcast is a must. It features interviews with insurtech founders and explores cutting-edge solutions.

Key Topics Covered:
- Parametric insurance for climate-related risks
- The role of big data in personalized policies
- How chatbots are improving customer service

5. The Climate Risk Podcast

Hosted by: Oliver Carpenter

Why Listen?
With natural disasters becoming more frequent, understanding climate risk is crucial for agents. This podcast explores how insurers are adapting.

Key Topics Covered:
- Flood insurance in high-risk zones
- Renewable energy coverage trends
- Government policies affecting disaster claims

6. The Future of Insurance

Hosted by: Bryan Falchuk

Why Listen?
Bryan Falchuk interviews industry leaders about where insurance is headed, from AI-driven underwriting to the gig economy’s impact on coverage needs.

Key Topics Covered:
- Usage-based insurance models
- The rise of embedded insurance
- How millennials are changing policy preferences

Podcasts for Personal & Professional Growth

7. The Savvy Agent Podcast

Hosted by: Ashley Fitzsimmons

Why Listen?
Geared toward female insurance professionals, this podcast covers work-life balance, leadership, and career growth.

Key Topics Covered:
- Overcoming imposter syndrome in sales
- Negotiation tactics for women in insurance
- Building a personal brand as an agent

8. The Producer’s Playbook

Hosted by: Alex Marren

Why Listen?
A high-energy podcast packed with sales strategies and motivational insights for insurance producers.

Key Topics Covered:
- Cold calling techniques that work in 2024
- How to close high-net-worth clients
- Time management for busy agents

Niche Insurance Podcasts Worth Checking Out

9. The Cyber Insurance Academy

Hosted by: Simon Oddy

Why Listen?
Cyber threats are skyrocketing, and this podcast helps agents understand the nuances of cyber liability coverage.

Key Topics Covered:
- Ransomware insurance trends
- How to assess a client’s cyber risk
- Regulatory compliance for cyber policies

10. The BenefitsPRO Podcast

Hosted by: Paul Wilson

Why Listen?
Focused on employee benefits and health insurance, this podcast is perfect for agents specializing in group coverage.

Key Topics Covered:
- Telemedicine’s impact on health plans
- Mental health benefits in the workplace
- The future of HSAs and FSAs

How to Get the Most Out of Insurance Podcasts

  • Listen During Commutes or Workouts – Turn downtime into learning opportunities.
  • Take Notes on Key Takeaways – Implement one new idea from each episode.
  • Engage with Hosts on Social Media – Many podcasters welcome listener questions.

The right podcast can sharpen your skills, expand your knowledge, and even inspire your next big move in the insurance world. Whether you’re looking for tech insights, sales strategies, or global risk trends, there’s a podcast on this list for you.
