Go Auto Insurance: How to Get a Defensive Driving Discount

Home / Blog / Blog Details

In today’s fast-paced world, where distracted driving and rising fuel costs dominate headlines, saving money on auto insurance feels like a small victory. One of the smartest ways to cut your premiums is by earning a defensive driving discount through Go Auto Insurance. But how exactly does it work? And why should you care in an era of inflation and economic uncertainty?

Why Defensive Driving Matters More Than Ever

With traffic congestion worsening and smartphone distractions at an all-time high, defensive driving isn’t just about saving money—it’s about staying safe. According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), over 3,000 people die annually in the U.S. due to distracted driving. Meanwhile, insurance companies like Go Auto reward drivers who take proactive steps to reduce risk.

The Financial Perks of Being a Safer Driver

Let’s face it: inflation is squeezing wallets everywhere. A defensive driving course could slash your premium by 5% to 20%, depending on your insurer and state regulations. For a policy costing $1,200 a year, that’s up to $240 back in your pocket—money better spent on groceries, gas, or even a weekend getaway.

How to Qualify for Go Auto’s Defensive Driving Discount

Not all insurers offer the same discounts, but Go Auto makes it straightforward. Here’s how to unlock savings while becoming a better driver.

Step 1: Find an Approved Defensive Driving Course

Go Auto typically accepts courses certified by state DMVs or national safety organizations like the National Safety Council (NSC). Options include:
- Online courses: Flexible and self-paced (ideal for busy schedules).
- In-person classes: Often hosted by local driving schools or community centers.
- Virtual live sessions: A hybrid option gaining popularity post-pandemic.

Pro tip: Before enrolling, confirm with Go Auto that the course qualifies for their discount.

Step 2: Complete the Course and Pass the Test

Most courses take 4 to 8 hours and cover:
- Accident avoidance techniques
- State-specific traffic laws
- The dangers of impaired/drowsy driving
- How to handle road rage (a growing issue in congested cities).

You’ll usually need to pass a final exam with a 70% or higher score.

Step 3: Submit Proof to Go Auto

After completing the course, you’ll receive a certificate of completion. Send this to Go Auto via:
- Their mobile app (hassle-free and eco-friendly).
- Email or customer portal.
- Fax or mail (for the old-school folks).

Discounts typically apply at your next renewal.

Beyond the Discount: How Defensive Driving Saves Lives

While saving money is great, the real win is reducing accidents. Consider these 2023 stats:
- 94% of crashes involve human error (NHTSA).
- Defensive drivers are 50% less likely to be in a collision (Insurance Institute for Highway Safety).

The Eco-Friendly Bonus

Did you know defensive driving can also lower your carbon footprint? Smooth acceleration and braking improve fuel efficiency by up to 35%, according to the U.S. Department of Energy. In a climate-conscious era, that’s a win-win.

Common Myths About Defensive Driving Discounts

Myth 1: “The Course Takes Too Much Time.”

Reality: Many online courses let you pause and resume. Finish during lunch breaks or while binge-watching Netflix.

Myth 2: “Only Seniors or High-Risk Drivers Need It.”

Reality: Even skilled drivers benefit. Insurance fraud and uninsured motorists are rising—defensive skills protect you.

Myth 3: “The Discount Isn’t Worth the Effort.”

Reality: Over 3 years, a 15% discount saves $500+ on average. That’s a new smartphone or a car repair fund.

Final Pro Tips to Maximize Savings

  1. Bundle discounts: Combine your defensive driving discount with multi-policy or good-student savings.
  2. Check state laws: Some states (like New York) mandate periodic courses for license renewal.
  3. Employer reimbursements: Certain jobs (e.g., delivery drivers) cover course fees.

In a world where every dollar counts, Go Auto’s defensive driving discount is a no-brainer. Stay safe, save money, and drive like the smartest person on the road—because you are.
